Privacy Policy

Welcome to Inkwell (“Inkwell”, “we”, “us” or “our”). Inkwell provides a communication service to create and share messages, images, and media through our messaging application.

  • Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in relation to our mobile services, web site, and any software provided on or in connection with Inkwell services (collectively the “Service”), and your choices about the collection and use of your information.

  • By using our Service you understand and agree that we are providing a platform for you to create and share content, including photos, messages, and other media (“User Content”), to the Service and to share User Content with your contacts.

  • Our Policy applies to all users who access the Service (“Users”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide us directly:

    • Your email address when you register for an Inkwell account through a Third Party Identity provider, and any other information requested by the application approved by you.

    • Profile information that you provide for your user profile (e.g., first and last name, picture, phone number). This information allows other people to identify you as a friend.

    • User Content (e.g., messages, photos, other media) that you send via the Service.

    • Communications between you and Inkwell.

  • Finding your friends on Inkwell:

    • If you choose, you can utilize our “Find Friends” feature to locate other people with Inkwell accounts through (i) your contacts list or (ii) third-party social media sites.

    • If you choose to find your friends through (i) your device’s contact list, then Inkwell will access your contacts list to determine whether any of your contacts are using Inkwell.

    • If you choose to find your friends through a (ii) third-party social media site, then you will be prompted to set up a link to the third-party service and you understand any information provided is governed by this Privacy Policy.

  • Cookies and similar technologies:

    • When you visit the Service, we may use cookies and similar technologies such as local storage to collect information about how you use Inkwell and to provide features to you.

  • Device identifiers:

    • When you use a mobile device like a tablet or phone to access our Service, we may access, collect, or store one or more “device identifiers” on your device or remotely. These identifiers are small data files that we can use to uniquely identify your device.

    • A device identifier may deliver information to us or to a third party about how you use the application, and some features of the Service may not function properly without these device identifiers.

  • Metadata:

    • Metadata is information that is associated with User Content but is not the content itself. Metadata may describe how, when, and by whom a piece of User Content was created and how that content is formatted.

2. How We Use Your Information

In addition to some of the specific uses of information we specify in this Privacy Policy, we may use information that we receive to

  • Help you efficiently access your information.

  • Remember information so you will not have to re-enter after exiting the Service.

  • Provide, improve, test, and monitor the effectiveness of our Service.

  • Diagnose or fix technological issues.

  • Automatically update the Inkwell application on your device.

3. Sharing of Your Information

Inkwell does not share any user content to any third-party providers such as ad agencies. We will not rent or sell your information to third parties outside Inkwell without your consent, except as noted in this Policy.

Parties with whom you may choose to share your User Content:

  • Any information or content that you voluntarily disclose for posting to the Service, such as User Content, becomes available to those it was shared with, as controlled by any applicable privacy settings that you set.

  • If you remove information that you posted to the Service, copies may remain viewable in cached and archived pages of the Service.

4. How We Store Your Information

Your information collected through the Service may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Inkwell, its Affiliates or Service Providers, maintain facilities.

5. Your Choices About Your Information

You can alter the information you provide at any time by logging in and changing your profile settings.

Following termination or deactivation of your account, Inkwell may retain information (including your profile information) and User Content for a commercially reasonable time for backup, archival, and/or audit purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

Inkwell does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register for the Service. The content produced by the Service are not directed at children under the age of 13.

7. How to Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Service, please contact us at inkwellcalpoly@gmail.com

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

Inkwell may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. We may provide you additional forms of notice of modifications or updates as appropriate under the circumstances. Your continued use of the Service after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification.
